Pokemon Gym Battle

Game Instructions

Choose a pokemon for your battle. The CPU will choose your opponent. Click on one of the attack buttons to battle.

If your attack is successful, you will gain experiene points and your opponent will lose health points. If you take a hit, you will lose health points.

If you lose all your health points, you lose. But, if you defeat your opponent, you will win the gym badge!
